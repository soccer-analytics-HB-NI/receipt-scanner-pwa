Privacy Policy for Receipt Scanner

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Receipt Images: We process receipt images locally in your browser using OCR technology. Images are not permanently stored on our servers.

Google Account Information: When you connect your Google account, we only access:

  • Basic profile information (name, email) for authentication
  • Read access to your Google Sheets list
  • Write access to selected Google Sheets for receipt data export

Receipt Data: Extracted text and structured data from receipts are stored locally in your browser and optionally exported to your chosen Google Sheet.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process receipt images to extract text and data
  • Export organized receipt data to your selected Google Sheets
  • Provide authentication and secure access to Google Services
  • Improve our OCR and parsing accuracy

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Processing: Receipt images are processed entirely in your browser using client-side OCR.

No Image Storage: We do not permanently store receipt images on our servers.

Secure Transmission: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.

Google OAuth: We use Google's secure OAuth 2.0 for authentication.

4. Google Sheets Integration

When you connect your Google account:

  • We request minimal necessary permissions
  • You control which Google Sheet receives your data
  • You can disconnect at any time
  • We don't access other Google services beyond Sheets

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sheets API: For exporting receipt data
  • Tesseract.js: Client-side OCR processing
  • Vercel: Hosting and serverless functions

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your Google account at any time
  • Delete locally stored receipt data
  • Request deletion of any data we may have
  • Access information about your data usage

7. Data Retention

We do not retain personal data beyond the active session. All receipt data is stored locally in your browser and in your Google Sheets under your control.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
